How Stoic works

STOICの仕組み

Other apps block the exit.
Stoic removes the reason to escape.

他のアプリは逃げ道を塞ぐ。

The momentForest · Opal · One SecStoic
The moment you reach for a social app. Access is blocked. No explanation is offered for the underlying urge — it remains intact and resurfaces the moment the block expires. Before granting access, Stoic presents one verified fact about how that platform is specifically engineered to capture attention. Behavioral research confirms that awareness of manipulation mechanisms significantly reduces their effectiveness. No competitor in this category has implemented this.
When the task ahead feels undefined. Outside their scope entirely. These apps assume you already know what you need to do — they restrict access to distractions, but do not address the ambiguity that drives you toward them. Stoic recognizes exactly what sends you to your phone in this moment — not temptation, but the paralysis of not knowing where to begin. Cognitive science identifies task ambiguity as the primary trigger of avoidance behavior. Rather than simply blocking your exit, Stoic stands alongside you and surfaces the one step you can take right now. When the path forward is clear, the need to escape dissolves on its own.
When the impulse to check your phone strikes. Suppression through willpower alone — a finite resource pitted against systems continuously refined by dedicated teams of thousands. TikTok alone employs over 1,500 engineers whose sole objective is maximizing time spent in-app. The asymmetry is structural, not personal. Stoic requires a 20-minute wait before access is granted. Neuroscience research consistently demonstrates that cravings, when not acted upon, peak and subside within 15 to 20 minutes. Leave the screen and the timer resets to zero. In the majority of cases, the impulse has passed before the wait concludes.
What the product is fundamentally built to do. Enforce compliance — treating impulses as the adversary and suppressing them by force. This model addresses symptoms rather than causes, and sustains a perpetual conflict between the user and their own behavior. Build genuine autonomy. Every mechanism — the awareness layer, the friction timer, the task structure — is designed to align your environment with the life you have chosen. Not to resist what you are trying to avoid, but to make what you actually want feel natural and attainable.

それぞれが、注意散漫のサイクルにおける異なる局面に対応しています。この4つはSTOICという統合システムを構成する要素の一部です。プラットフォームが数兆円をかけて構築してきた構造的優位に、正面から対抗するために設計された行動設計アーキテクチャを形成しています。

01

Before you unlock

ロック解除の前に

Before you can open TikTok or Instagram, Stoic quietly shows you one fact about how that platform is designed to capture your attention. Not a lecture. Just the information you were never meant to have — at the only moment you can act on it. No competitor has built this.

TikTokやInstagramを開こうとする瞬間、STOICはそのプラットフォームがあなたの注意をどう設計しているかを、静かに一つだけ提示します。説教でも批判でもなく、意識的な選択ができる唯一の瞬間に届ける情報を。これを実装している競合は存在しません。

02

The 20-minute pause

20分、待ってみる

When you feel the urge to open a social app, Stoic asks you to wait 20 minutes before the lock releases. Leave the screen and the timer resets to zero. Neuroscience confirms: the average craving lasts about that long. Most of the time, by the time the wait ends, the urge is already gone.

SNSを開きたいと感じた瞬間、STOICは20分間の待機を求めます。画面を離れるとタイマーはリセット。神経科学が示す衝動の平均持続時間は約20分。ほとんどの場合、待つだけで衝動は消えています。

03

Your next move, always clear

次の一手を、常に明確に

Most SNS use is not addiction — it is escape from ambiguity. When you do not know where to start, anxiety builds and your phone becomes the easiest exit. Stoic removes that exit: one specific next step, always surfaced and ready.

多くのSNS利用は「依存」ではなく、曖昧さからの「逃避」です。何から始めるかわからないとき、不安が生まれ、スマートフォンが最も簡単な逃げ場になります。STOICのパートナーシステムは、その根本——曖昧さ——を取り除きます。

04

Instant recognition of progress

前進した実感を、すぐに得る

Every time you complete a step, Stoic responds immediately. Not a badge or streak — just the clean, direct sensation of having moved forward. Faster and more reliable than any notification your phone could send.

ステップを完了するたびに、STOICはすぐに応えます。バッジでも連続記録でもなく、一歩進んだという純粋な感覚です。スマートフォンのどんな通知よりも速く、確実に届きます。

The uncomfortable truth

不都合な現実

Every time you open Instagram, TikTok, or X —
you enter a behavioral experiment.

Instagram、TikTok、X を開くたびに、
あなたは行動実験の被験者になっています。

The platform observes your actions. Measures your response time. Records what made you stop scrolling — and what made you keep going. It updates its model of you, in real time, with one goal: keep you inside for one more second.

You are not a user. You are a subject.

Your restlessness, your boredom, your anxiety — these are not side effects. They are product features. Every second of your attention is revenue.

We are living inside the largest attention extraction experiment in human history. Most of us never consciously agreed to it.

Behind these platforms is the combined intelligence of thousands of engineers and hundreds of billions of dollars in investment — Meta alone spends over $38 billion on R&D every year. Algorithms built at this scale operate with a force that far exceeds individual willpower. The cruelest part: most people never realize they are being controlled. That is how complete the system has become.

あなたをアプリ内にもう1秒留めることだけを目的に、
リアルタイムであなたのモデルを更新し続けています。

あなたは「ユーザー」ではありません。実験対象者です。

落ち着きのなさ、退屈、不安——これらは副作用ではありません。製品の仕様です。あなたの注意の1秒1秒が、収益になっています。

私たちは今、人類史上最大規模の注意収奪実験の中にいます。そのほとんどの人が、同意した覚えを持っていません。

これらのシステムの背後には、何千人もの専任エンジニアと数兆円規模の投資がある——メタ社だけで年間5兆円超の研究開発費を注ぎ込んでいる。この規模で磨かれたアルゴリズムは、個人の意志など遥かに超える力で行動を制御する。皮肉なことに、制御されていることにすら、私たちは気づくことができない。それがこのシステムの完成度だ。
